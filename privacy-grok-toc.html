Privacy Policy

The Grok TOC Panel extension is committed to respecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

Data Collected

No data is collected. The extension only reads visible content on grok.com to generate a local Table of Contents (TOC) for your convenience.

External Communication

This extension does not send data anywhere. It operates entirely on your browser without connecting to any external servers or services.

Permissions

The extension only requests access to *://*.grok.com/* to function properly. It does not access or monitor any other websites.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the email provided in the Chrome Web Store listing.

Thank you for using Grok TOC Panel!
