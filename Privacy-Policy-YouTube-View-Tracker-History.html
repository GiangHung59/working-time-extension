Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to YouTube View Tracker History! This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our extension. We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring the confidentiality and security of your information.

Data Collection

Our extension does not collect any personal or identifiable information. We only track data related to your YouTube activity, including the number of times you watch a video and when you first viewed it. This data is stored locally in your browser and is not sent to any external servers.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To store your video watch history locally on your browser.
  • Scripting: To run scripts necessary for tracking and processing your YouTube activity.
  • ActiveTab: To interact with the currently open YouTube tab.
  • Tabs: To track the video currently being viewed in the browser tab.

Security

Your privacy and security are important to us. All the data tracked by the extension is stored locally in your browser, which means it is not accessible to third parties. No data is shared or transmitted externally.
