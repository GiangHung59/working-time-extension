Introduction

This privacy policy outlines how the ChatGPT & Grok TOC extension handles your data. Our commitment is to your privacy and data protection.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or identifiable information. All data processing is performed locally within your browser.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save user preferences such as visibility state of the table of contents.
  • Content Scripts: To inject necessary scripts for generating table of contents on supported domains.

Scope

The extension is only active on the following domains:

  • https://chatgpt.com/*
  • *://*.grok.com/*

Security

Your data remains on your device. No data is sent externally or shared with third parties. The extension is built with privacy-first principles.
